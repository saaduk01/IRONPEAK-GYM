OUR STORY

Built by Athletes.
Designed for Everyone.

IRONPEAK was born in 2016 from a single vision: to give Karachi a gym that doesn't just sell memberships — it transforms lives. Founded by ex-professional bodybuilder Usman Raza, our facility in DHA Phase 6 has become the go-to destination for serious fitness in the city.

We operate on one principle: train hard, recover smart, repeat. Whether you're stepping into a gym for the first time or preparing for a competition, our 12,000 sq ft facility and dedicated coaching staff are built to meet you exactly where you are.
